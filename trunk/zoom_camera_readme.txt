Overview
--------
Description: External Zoom Camera.
Availability: Tech level 10+
Cost: 10000 Cr
Use: Press 'b' (lower case 'b') to cycle between the modes.
Activation: Press 'n' (lower case 'n').
Modes: Activate, then on the external view...
    Zoom In
    Zoom Out
    Deactivate (can be used on regular view)
USP: Uses your last view direction before you go to the external view.

Requirements
------------
    Oolite 1.77+

Install
-------
After unzipping, move or copy the folder, "Zoom_Camera_1.0.oxp", into your AddOns directory.

Uninstall
---------
Delete the directory, "Zoom_Camera_1.0.oxp", from your AddOns directory.
